My research in Theoretical Astrophysics is focused on the the study of the dynamics in dense stellar systems known as globular clusters. In particular, I aim at finding possible dynamical signatures for the presence of intermediate-mass black holes.

From 2016, I am a PhD candidate at the University of Melbourne, where I work under the supervision of Dr. Michele Trenti . Before starting my PhD, I received an italian scholarship to collaborate with Dr. Glenn van de Ven at the Max Planck Institute of Astronomy (Heidelberg, Germany).
